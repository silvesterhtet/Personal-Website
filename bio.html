About Me

Computers and technology have been my passion ever since I had access to my dad's old laptop. I loved that I was able to play games, watch videos and learn about anything I wanted.

My introduction to programming came when in the fifth grade, when my Computer Literacy class introduced me to Scratch. I was fascinated by the idea that I could create my own games and animations. I remember spending countless hours making games, testing them, and sharing them with my friends.

I have come a long way since then, and I am now a Computer Science student at UC Berkeley learning how to develop apps, design an OS and hack into vulnerable systems. I am drawn by the challenges and opporunities that come with the field and hope to keep doing what I love.
